My Indoor Plants

Our Plant wall

Indoor house plants offer a myriad of benefits that go beyond mere aesthetics such as reducing stress levels as they can lead to feelings of comfort, soothing and naturalness. Therapeutic effects for individuals dealing with mental health symptoms, indoor gardening can be therapeutic. Horticultural therapy has been used to improve well-being among people with depression, anxiety, dementia, and other conditions. Along with better air quality for your home, houseplants act as natural air purifiers by removing toxins and releasing oxygen. Breathing cleaner air can have long-term health benefits.
